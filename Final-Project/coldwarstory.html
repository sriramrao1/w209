Olympics as a proxy for World Order

Olympics during the Cold War

Soviet Union renounced all events that were seen as tools of capitalism, including the Olympics. Post World War, Soviet Union saw the Olympics as a means to display Soviet dominance to the world as well as to their own population.

In 1980, following USSR's invasion of Afghanistan, USA led 60 other countries in boycotting the Moscow Olympics. USSR swept gold medals in mos events

In 1984, Soviet Government fearing backlash from US protesters, led 13 other communist nations and boycotted the Los Angeles Olympics.
