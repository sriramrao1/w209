Olympics as a proxy for World Order

The Rise of China

China was admitted in to the IOC in 1979

China boycotted the 1980 over the USSR's invasion of Afghanistan

China has been a rising Olympic power starting with 76 medals in 1984 to 184 medals in 2008 at the Beijing Olympics. With that tally, China ranked second in the overall medal count, topping traditional Olympic super powers such as Russia, Germany and Japan
